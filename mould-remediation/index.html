Why Mould Can't Be Ignored

Auckland's Climate Makes Your Home a Target for Mould

Auckland's high humidity, frequent rain, and older housing stock create ideal conditions for mould growth. What starts as a small patch can quickly spread behind walls, under floors, and into ceiling cavities.

Respiratory Health Risks

Mould spores trigger asthma, allergies, coughing, and sinus infections. Black mould produces mycotoxins that are harmful with prolonged exposure.

Hidden Contamination

Visible mould is often just the surface. Colonies thrive inside wall cavities, under flooring, and in subfloor spaces where you can't see them.

Property Damage

Mould degrades plasterboard, timber, carpet, and soft furnishings. Left untreated, remediation costs escalate and property value drops.
