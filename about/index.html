Who We Are

A Full-Service Restoration Company Based in Auckland

iDry Restorations is a full-service water damage restoration company located in Auckland. Our certified team of restoration experts specialises in bringing your belongings back to their pre-loss condition following a fire, flood, or other disaster.

Our flood restoration service was started over 7 years ago. Since then, we have grown at an explosive rate, becoming the fastest growing restoration company in New Zealand. This growth led to the creation of the iDry Restorations division, dedicated to providing rapid, professional disaster recovery services to Auckland homeowners and businesses.

Our job is only done when you are comfortable and satisfied with your property again. You are always the top priority of every project we undertake.
